Baidyanath Dham (Deoghar)

Baidyanath Dham (Deoghar) is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva and a sacred pilgrimage site in India. The temple is also known as Baba Baidyanath Temple and holds immense importance in Hindu mythology. Every year, millions of devotees visit during the Shravani Mela, carrying holy water from the Ganga to offer to Lord Shiva. It is considered a place of ultimate faith, devotion, and spiritual fulfillment.
