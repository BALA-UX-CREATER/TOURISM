Rajrappa Temple (Ramgarh)

Rajrappa Temple (Ramgarh) is a famous Shakti Peeth in Jharkhand, dedicated to Goddess Chhinnamasta, a unique and rare form of Maa Shakti. The temple is situated at the confluence of the Bhairavi and Damodar rivers, surrounded by scenic hills and forests. Devotees believe that worship here fulfills wishes and brings prosperity. The temple also attracts visitors for its natural beauty, boating, and the spiritual energy of the place.
