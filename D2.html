Parasnath Hill (Shikharji, Giridih)

Parasnath Hill (Shikharji, Giridih) is the highest peak in Jharkhand and one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites for Jains. It is believed that 20 out of 24 Jain Tirthankaras attained salvation here, making it a holy destination. The hill is surrounded by lush forests, offering both spiritual peace and natural beauty. Pilgrims trek to the summit, where temples and shrines dedicated to Tirthankaras are located. It is a blend of devotion, heritage, and breathtaking landscapes.
