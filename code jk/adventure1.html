Khandoli Hill

Located in Giridih district, Khandoli Hill is famous for its adventurous activities and scenic beauty around Khandoli Dam and Lake. From rock climbing to boating, it offers thrilling experiences with stunning views.

Travel Information

  • Location: Giridih, Jharkhand
  • Hotels: Resorts & lodges within 3–5 km
  • Transport: Taxi, Bus, Auto available
  • Best Season: October – March
  • Adventure: Rock climbing, boating, zipline
