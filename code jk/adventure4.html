Netarhat (Queen of Chotanagpur)

Netarhat, known as the Queen of Chotanagpur, is a serene hill station renowned for its breathtaking sunrise and sunset views at Magnolia Point and Sunrise Point. The region offers trekking to the beautiful Ghaghri Falls, nature walks, and photography in dense pine forests.

Travel Information

  • Location: Latehar district, Jharkhand
  • Hotels: Forest lodges, guest houses, and hotels nearby
  • Transport: Nearest railway station: Ranchi (156 km)
  • Best Season: October – March (ideal for cool weather & treks)
  • Activities: Trekking, nature walks, photography, forest exploration
  • Emergency: +91-9431200000 (local helpline)
