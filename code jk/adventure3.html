Parasnath Hill

Parasnath Hill, the highest peak in Jharkhand, is both a sacred pilgrimage site for Jains and a haven for trekkers. Known as Shikharji, the trek to the summit is challenging but rewarding, offering panoramic views and spiritual peace. The nearby Satpahar hills also host adventure sports like paragliding.

Travel Information

  • Location: Giridih district, Jharkhand
  • Hotels: Lodges and hotels available in Giridih & Madhuban
  • Transport: Nearest railway station: Parasnath (20 km away)
  • Best Season: October – March (pleasant weather for trekking)
  • Guides: Local trekking & pilgrimage guides available
