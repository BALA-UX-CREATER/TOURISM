Hazaribagh wildlife sanctuary

Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary, located in Jharkhand, is a haven for diverse flora and fauna amidst scenic hills and dense forests. It is home to animals like tigers, leopards, sambar, nilgai, chital, and wild boar. The sanctuary also hosts a variety of birds, making it a delight for birdwatchers. Surrounded by picturesque landscapes, it offers a peaceful escape for nature lovers. It is one of the oldest sanctuaries in India, preserving rich biodiversity and natural beauty.
