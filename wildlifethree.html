Mahuadanr Wolf Sanctuary

Mahuadanr Wolf Sanctuary in Jharkhand is India’s only sanctuary dedicated to wolves, making it a truly unique destination for wildlife lovers. Spread across the lush forests and rolling hills of Latehar district, it offers a natural habitat for a significant population of Indian wolves. Visitors can witness the wolves roaming freely along with other wildlife like jackals, hyenas, and deer. The sanctuary is also rich in biodiversity, with dense forests and scenic landscapes adding to its charm. It is a perfect blend of adventure, nature, and rare wildlife conservation.

Travel Information

  • Location: Latehar Bus Stand → Mahuadanr Wolf Sanctuary
  • Hotels: Naturehat Resort, Parwati Rest House, Muskan Hotel
  • Transport: Bus, Taxi, Auto available
