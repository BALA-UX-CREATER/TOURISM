Hundru Falls

Hundru Falls, formed on the Subarnarekha River, is one of the most famous waterfalls in Jharkhand and a major adventure tourism spot near Ranchi. Surrounded by rugged cliffs, rocky trails, and caves, it offers thrilling trekking, rock climbing, rappelling, and exploration opportunities for adventure seekers.

Travel Information

  • Location: 45 km from Ranchi, on Subarnarekha River
  • Hotels: Available in Ranchi (budget to luxury)
  • Transport: Well connected by road; nearest railway station: Ranchi
  • Best Season: July – October (monsoon & post-monsoon)
  • Guides: Local trekking & adventure guides available
