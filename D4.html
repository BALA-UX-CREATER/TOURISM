Angrabari Temple Complex (Amreshwar Dham, Khunti)

The Angrabari Temple Complex, also known as Amreshwar Dham, is a famous spiritual site located in Khunti district of Jharkhand. Dedicated primarily to Lord Shiva, the complex also houses temples of Lord Ganesha, Goddess Parvati, and Lord Hanuman. It is surrounded by natural greenery, giving the place a peaceful and divine atmosphere. The temple is maintained by the Ramakrishna Mission, which ensures cleanliness and spiritual discipline in the area. This place attracts a large number of devotees, especially during Shravan month and Maha Shivaratri.
